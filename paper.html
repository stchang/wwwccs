Advisor's Statement

This paper describes a new characterization of lazy evaluation that improves on existing models by following machine implementations more closely. Stephen proposed the idea and we jointly worked out the final semantics. Stephen then proved the essential properties and correctness of the system.

I wrote the introduction and Stephen wrote the rest of the paper. We then jointly edited the paper into its final form.
